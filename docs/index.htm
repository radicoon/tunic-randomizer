TUNIC Randomizer

An extensive randomizer mod for TUNIC, an action-adventure game about a tiny fox in a big world. Featuring randomization of every item in the game, as well as enemy randomization, enhanced fox customization, custom items, and much, much more!

Game Modes

Choose between a classic randomizer experience or Hexagon Quest.

Classic Randomizer

Find the three Hexagon Keys and Take Your Rightful Place or Share Your Wisdom to finish.

Hexagon Quest

30 Gold Hexagons are shuffled throughout the world. Find 20 of them and visit The Heir to finish.

Note: Enemy randomization is not currently supported in this mode.

Logic Options

Customize your randomizer experience to your liking. Must be set before starting a new game.

Sword Progression

Replaces the stick / swords in the item pool with four "Sword Upgrades" that progressively level up.

Shuffle Abilities

Locks the ability to pray, use the Holy Cross*, and use the Ice Rod combo until the respective manual pages are found.

Keys Behind Bosses

Choose if the three Hexagon Keys are randomly shuffled or placed behind their respective boss fights.

Start with Sword

The player will spawn with a Sword in the inventory on New Game. Does not count towards Sword Progression.

Other Settings

These options can be changed at any time throughout the game.

Easier Heir Fight

Attacks deal additional damage to The Heir based on the total number of checks found.

Cheaper Shop Items

Reduces the cost of the four randomized Shop items from 1000 to 300. Does not affect other Shop items.

Bonus Upgrades

Makes the "golden treasure" items give a bonus level-up to certain stats on pick-up (not applied retroactively).

Chest Interruption

Enemies will not be able to interrupt you while opening chests if this option is turned on.

Fool Traps

Enables traps that replace low-value money rewards and damage or add negative effects.

???

Turn on this setting for a nice, if not nauseating, surprise!

Hint Settings

Optional settings that add hints for progress to the game.

Path of the Hero

Places a major hint at specific locations around the world, including the Mailbox, the Hero's Graves, and the plaque in the Sealed Temple. These hint towards major progression items, such as Magic Items, Laurels, Hexagons, and more.

Ghost Foxes

Spawns 15 Ghost Foxes around the world that give minor hints. These hints include the locations of useful non-progression items, items in hard-to-reach locations, and barren locations. There are over 50 unique Ghost Fox spawns, all with their own custom dialogue!

Items and Chests Match

All freestanding items will have their model swapped to appear as the item they are randomized as. Chest textures will be swapped to match the item inside. This includes Fairies, Treasures, the three Hexagons, and the Hero's Laurels.
